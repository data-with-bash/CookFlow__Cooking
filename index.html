CookFlow

Warm-up micro app for meal planning

AI micro app pattern

Generate a cooking to-do list from your actual day.

This single-screen app creates a meal plan, grocery list, substitutions, and budget feasibility logic. It is designed as a hackathon-ready flow: clear input, structured output, and an easy demo story.

Comma-separated pantry items make the grocery logic smarter.
